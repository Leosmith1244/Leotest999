Public Speaking Resources

Recommended Books

  • "Talk Like TED" by Carmine Gallo
  • "The Art of Public Speaking" by Dale Carnegie
  • "Resonate" by Nancy Duarte
  • "Steal the Show" by Michael Port
  • "Confessions of a Public Speaker" by Scott Berkun

Recommended Videos

"How to Speak So People Want to Listen" - Julian Treasure "Your Body Language Shapes Who You Are" - Amy Cuddy "How Great Leaders Inspire Action" - Simon Sinek "The Secret Structure of Great Talks" - Nancy Duarte

Speaking Tips

  • Know your audience and tailor your message
  • Practice multiple times, including out loud
  • Time your speech and leave room for pauses
  • Prepare notes or an outline, not a script

  • Make eye contact with different audience members
  • Use purposeful gestures and movement
  • Vary your vocal tone, pace, and volume
  • Pause for emphasis and to collect your thoughts

  • Start with a strong opening to grab attention
  • Organize with clear structure and transitions
  • Use stories and examples to illustrate points
  • End with a memorable conclusion
