Meeting Format

Our meetings follow a structured format designed to maximize learning opportunities.

Prepared Speeches

Members deliver speeches from Toastmasters Pathways or other educational programs.

Table Topics

Impromptu speaking session to practice thinking on your feet.

Evaluations

Constructive feedback helps speakers identify strengths and areas for improvement.

Reports

Timer, Grammarian, and Ah-Counter provide insights on meeting dynamics.
