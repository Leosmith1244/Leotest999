What to Expect as a Guest

As a guest, you’ll observe a friendly, structured meeting with prepared speeches, impromptu speaking (Table Topics), and evaluations. You’re welcome to participate in Table Topics if you’d like, but there’s no pressure. Arrive a few minutes early to meet members!
