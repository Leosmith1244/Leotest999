Club Officer Responsibilities

  • Presides at club meetings and executive committee meetings
  • Represents the club at area, division, and district events
  • Ensures the club meets Toastmasters International quality standards
  • Appoints committee chairs and assists with their responsibilities
  • Works with club officers to set and achieve club goals

  • Plans club meetings and assigns meeting roles
  • Helps members complete projects and tracks their progress
  • Orders and distributes educational materials
  • Coordinates speech contests and special meetings
  • Mentors new members and helps them get started

  • Conducts membership-building programs
  • Greets guests and helps them feel welcome
  • Processes membership applications
  • Maintains accurate membership records
  • Works with VP Public Relations to promote the club

  • Promotes the club through social media and other channels
  • Creates and distributes club newsletters
  • Maintains the club website and online presence
  • Seeks publicity opportunities in local media
  • Coordinates open houses and special events

  • Records and distributes meeting minutes
  • Maintains club records and historical documents
  • Handles club correspondence
  • Updates the club constitution and bylaws as needed
  • Submits officer lists to Toastmasters International

  • Manages club bank accounts and financial records
  • Collects membership dues and other payments
  • Prepares financial reports for club meetings
  • Submits payments to Toastmasters International
  • Prepares an annual budget for the club

  • Arranges the meeting room and equipment
  • Greets members and guests at meetings
  • Maintains club property and supplies
  • Manages meeting logistics and timing
  • Assists with special events and contests
